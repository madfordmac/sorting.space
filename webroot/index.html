When given multiple options, humans have a hard time giving each item its due attention. But the problem can be made simpler. If you've ever sorted an Excel spreadsheet or ordered your Amazon results with the least expensive items first, you've had a computer use a sorting algorithm ‚Äî breaking the list down into many comparisons of just two items. That works well for people, too. It's much easier to pick your favorite of two items than your favorite of 3 or more.

And it is that simple:

  1. Put your things in the list on the left.
  2. Click "Start sorting!"
  3. Pick the winner from each pair of items that appears.
  4. The ordered list will appear after you've made all your choices.
